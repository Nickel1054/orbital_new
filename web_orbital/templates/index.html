Welcome to Orbiter project!


This app allows you to plot orbits and current positions of planets, asteroids, comets and other bodies of the Solar System (even non-existent!).

The calculations use Keplerian elements (aka Orbital elements) to calculate position of an object. By expanding this calculation to the whole sidereal period we are building an orbit, connecting positions of every 360 position coordinates - each build with a step of 1/360 of a orbital period.
